Our History

Established in 1959, Member of Caritas Internationalis since 1965, Caritas Rwanda is a national world wide Organization operating through its network of 10 Diocesan Caritas.

We believe every human being carries inherent dignity that no crisis, poverty, or conflict can ever erase.

  Program Area

Social Welfare

Community mobilization, support and advocacy for the most vulnerable people — providing safety nets, emergency assistance, and dignity-restoring support systems for Rwanda’s most vulnerable families and communities.

  • Community support & social protection
  • Emergency humanitarian response
  • Social advocacy & inclusion programs
